## Quick Overview

On Limitless, you can **buy and sell shares** representing future event outcomes (e.g. "Will BTC reach $100k this week?").

- Shares are **always priced between &#36;0.00 and &#36;1.00** USDC
- Every pair of "YES" + "NO" shares is fully collateralized by &#36;1.00 USDC
- Shares are created when **opposing sides come to an agreement on odds** — the sum of what each side is willing to pay equals &#36;1.00
- Winning shares pay out **&#36;1.00**. Losing shares are worthless after market resolution.
